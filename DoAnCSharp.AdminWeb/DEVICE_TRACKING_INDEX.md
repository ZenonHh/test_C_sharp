## 💡 Key Concepts

### Device Tracking
Automatic capture of device info (phone, model, OS) when QR scanned

### Online Status
Real-time tracking of whether device is currently online

### Heartbeat
Keep-alive signal to maintain device online status

### API Endpoints
REST endpoints to get online devices and dashboard stats
